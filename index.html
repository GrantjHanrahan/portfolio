About Me

I am a Web Developer that has just graduated from the 12 week Full-Time Full Stack Web Development Course at General Assembly.

I have 3+ years experience in Customer Service as well as 12 months experience as a Youth Worker.

My interest in coding began as a result of completing online tutorials which led me to complete a 12 month part-time Certificate IV in Programming at Tafe NSW. I then sought to expand upon my skillset by completing the General Assembly Full Stack Web Development Immersive.

I am also passionate about working with young people to facilitate their personal, social and educational development. I am working towards being able to combine my two passions so that I can introduce young people to coding, particularly those from disadvantaged backgrounds.
